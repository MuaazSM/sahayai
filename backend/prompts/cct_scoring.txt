Analyze the provided conversation message from a patient who may have cognitive impairment. Score their cognitive performance across 6 dimensions.

Scoring scale: 0.0 (severe impairment) to 1.0 (perfectly normal).

Dimensions:
- recall_accuracy: Can they reference recent events, names, or facts correctly? Do they confuse details?
- response_latency_indicator: Is their speech fluent? Short fragmented responses suggest slower processing. Flowing natural speech = high score.
- vocabulary_richness: Are they using varied, contextually appropriate words? Or repeating the same few words?
- temporal_orientation: Do they know roughly what time of day, what day, where they are?
- narrative_coherence: Does their message follow a logical thread? Or jump between topics, trail off, or loop?
- semantic_consistency: Is what they're saying consistent with known facts and what they said earlier?

Calibration:
- Healthy elderly: 0.75-0.90
- Mild cognitive impairment: 0.55-0.75
- Moderate impairment: 0.35-0.55
- Severe impairment: below 0.35
- Nobody scores perfect 1.0

Return ONLY valid JSON, no explanation:
{"recall_accuracy": 0.0, "response_latency_indicator": 0.0, "vocabulary_richness": 0.0, "temporal_orientation": 0.0, "narrative_coherence": 0.0, "semantic_consistency": 0.0, "overall_score": 0.0, "notes": "brief observation"}
